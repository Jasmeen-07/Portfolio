About Me

Hello! I’m Jasmeen Kaur, an IT support specialist who enjoys solving problems, supporting users, and making sure everything works the way it should. I’ve always been drawn to technology because I like understanding how things work and helping others when they don’t.

More than anything, I take pride in being reliable, detail-oriented, and easy to work with. I’m looking for roles where I can keep growing, contribute to a strong IT team, and be someone people trust when things go wrong.
